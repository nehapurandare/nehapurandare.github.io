More about the things I do

Texas Royals

A spirit group focused on the four pillars of sisterhood, spirit, social, and service. Our philanthropy deals with Alzheimer's Research and fundraising. We are also very oriented in volunteering around the Austin community while spreading UT spirit!

College Tuition Budget Advisory Committee (CTBAC)

A committee focused on analyzing and evaluating the current allocations of student tuition and conveying the information to the students and faculty of McCombs. We conduct multiple surveys to gather sentiments from faculty and students about what they would like to see changed in McCombs and relay the information to the UT President.

University Finance Association (UFA)

An organization focused on bridging the gap between the academic and professional lives of aspiring business students. As Director of Corporate Relations I am able to interact directly with corporations and help exposue students to the various facets of finance.
